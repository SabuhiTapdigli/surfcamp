Overview

14 Days of Surf Lessons

No accommodation

4 hours per day of surfing in a group with instructors with 1 session of functional training in dedicated Drysurf gym and 2 photo shootings. All equipment is included. Suitable for all levels.

Classes

Students learn surfing in small groups with instructors, suitable for all levels.

  • Small groups of max 8 students per instructor
  • All surf equipment is included
  • Lessons are in Spanish and English
  • Two photo shootings 10:00 - 12:00 on Tuesdays and Fridays
  • 1 day of functional training in Drysurf gym
Transport

Students gather at the school before class, get wetsuit and surf board. Then:

  • Students get to the van and school drives the group to the beach
  • Surf theory and practice at the beach from 10:00 to 12:00
  • 30 mins break at the beach
  • Second session of surf theory and practice at the beach from 12:30 to 14:30
  • Students get back in the van and school drives everyone back
Accomodation

Accommodation is not provided. You'll need to find a place to stay in Lanzarote yourself.

Food

School does not provide food, but it is recommended to take food and something to drink, for a break between sessions during the day. Also there are plenty of cafes and restaurants in the area where you can enjoy dinner after your surf day

Airport transfer

You can take a taxi or rent a car at the airport for an affordable price. Otherwise, please check the availability of the buses to drive to Famara.

What’s Include

At SoulSurf, we are dedicated to providing you with an unforgettable and enriching surf experience. Our all-inclusive package ensures that you have everything you need for an incredible time by the waves. When you choose our surf camp, you can look forward to the following inclusions:

  • Daily Surf Lessons:Enjoy daily surf lessons led by our experienced and certified instructors. Whether you're a beginner or looking to improve your skills, our lessons cater to all levels.
  • Top-Quality Equipment:We provide high-quality surfboards and wetsuits tailored to your skill level and preferences, ensuring you have the best gear for an optimal experience
  • Accommodation:Relax and recharge in comfortable and well-equipped accommodation options. Choose from shared dorms or private rooms, all designed for your comfort
  • Nutritious Meals:Start your day with a delicious and hearty breakfast. Our meal plan includes healthy options to keep you energized throughout your surf sessions.
  • Transportation:We offer convenient transportation to and from the surf spots, making it hassle-free for you to focus on catching waves and enjoying the ocean.
  • Surf Theory Workshops:Enhance your understanding of surfing techniques and ocean awareness with our informative surf theory workshops.
  • Guided Surf Sessions:Join guided surf sessions with our instructors who will be in the water with you, providing real-time feedback and assistance.
  • Safety First:Your safety is our priority. We provide comprehensive safety briefings and have lifeguards on duty during surf sessions.
  • Environmental Stewardship: We are committed to preserving the beauty of our oceans. Join us in beach clean-up initiatives and eco-friendly practices.
  • 24/7 Support: Our team is here to ensure your experience is seamless. From booking to departure, we offer 24/7 support for any questions or assistance you need.
